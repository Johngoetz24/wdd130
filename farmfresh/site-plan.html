Overview

Purpose

The purpose of ths website is to provide our audience an idea of what to expect from their local farmers market. It will show the different vendors and possible products which could be bought at the farmers market. It will also have a section to let users see what events and entertainment the local farmers market will have.

Audience

The intended audience would be those who are looking for specific local goods provided by vendors and those who are interested in enjoying some local entertainment.

Content

Home page

Why will people come to your site? Are you looking for healthier local options? This Farmers market site takes all the searching and puts it into one easy to use website. This website will help you see what vendors are selling, what fun activities are open to you, and the best times to visit your local market. It will have everything laid out in a simple readabily way, clarifying what users should expect when they visit the market. This site will be able to help users buy event tickets and help users understand the farmer market experience they will receive if they decided they would like to attend. What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? Common question of dates and time will be in a easy to read format. The site would also clearly state where the Farmers market it being held so that visitors will not be confused. There will be a section that covers all the special events and explains what they are, when they are, and how much it will cost. The site will also answer the visitors questions on when vendors will be at the market and the basic items they sell. There would also be a planned map of where the main events will take place and where the vendors will be at. There will be sections on the site which explains what the overall experience at the market will be like. There will be reviews so that people know exactly what they are getting by choosing to attend this farmers market. If a visitor has more questions, there will also be different links which would take them to other sites that go more indepth of what they are looking for. What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? Some activities vistors will be able to do is buy tickets for events that are happening. Also they will be able to find links connecting them to social media, blogs, and other information correlating with the farmers market. For visitors who want to attend the farmers market and are thinking of spending a lot of money, they can put in a request for a wagon to carry all the goods they buy from the local vendors. The users of the site will also be able to look at the local vendors who are going to be at the market and know find out what they would be selling. If the user is looking for something specific they will be able to search in specific categories to more easily find the items they are looking for. What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? If a user want to place an order for tickets they would have to enter in a name, credit card information, and a specific day for the event they want to attend. When a user places an order for a wagon, they are going to need to put in a name and time of day they would like to borrow the wagon. If the user wants to search for specific an item, they would need to select the type of item and the site would give them possible vendor who would have the item they are looking for. Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? This site will streamline the process of ordering ticket, reserving wagons, and understand what is at the Farmers market. While there will be different site which talk about this farmers market, this site will consolidate all the most useful information for the user. This site will also provide the most up to date information on what will be at the farmers market allowing users to stay in the loop of everything. The simplicity and information of this site is what is what will make it more useful to visitors than any other site. The best part of this site is how everything will be linked to more sources if the user is looking for more information. This part of the site will be talking more about the experience you will have at the farmers market. It will also provide most of the key information found from the questions above.
