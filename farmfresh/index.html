Welcome to the Market!

At the Farmers Market, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant community by connecting people to local, sustainable food and unique products. Through curated social events and dynamic experiences, we showcase the hard work and creativity of our vendors, while building a space where neighbors come together to celebrate, shop, and share in the joy of fresh, locally grown produce and handmade goods.

If you are looking for some local fun, some of our local events are for you! Every week we run three different activites ranging from firework shows to food parties. Come see what events we have to offer!

More Questions?

Where are we located?

We are located at Main and Sesame street. We have parking next to our enterance and we are open everyday from 7am to 7pm.

Are you cash only?

All of our vendors take cash and card. All card transcations will have a .4% fee. If you need cash also have two ATM machines on site.

Are you pet friendly?

Your family is our family! We would love to have all of your family members (including your pet) to join us. As long as your pet is on a leash, you are more than welcome to bring them!
