Commonly Asked Questions

When should I book my apartment?

As soon as possible! Most apartment complexes will give you a discount as long as you reserve your spot two months before the semester begins. Also a lot of complexes book up quickly so it would be better to book your spot when they have space.

What should I do about parking?

Call your complex as soon as possible. There are limited parking spots, so it would be best to check with your complex if they have any parking spots available.

How can I room with my friends?

Friends are family! All apartment complexes want to room you with your firends. Just give a call after you book an room and request that your room will be filled with your friend!
